Privacy Policy

Playlist Radio Generator is powered by the Spotify Web API and is developed as an open-source application. By using this app, you agree to the use of your Spotify account username and data related to your playlists and tracks.

Playlist Radio Generator does not store, collect, or share any of the data used. All information is used solely for generating customized Spotify playlists based on your preferences.

While your data is not stored or used maliciously, if you wish to revoke Playlist Radio Generator's permissions, you can do so by visiting your apps page and clicking "REMOVE ACCESS" for Playlist Radio Generator.

For a more detailed guide on revoking access, please refer to this detailed guide.
